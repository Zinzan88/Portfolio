Zinzan's Portfolio

I am currently studying Games Computing at Northumbria University, in May I will graduate with a First Class BSc.

Throughout my life I have been enamoured with gaming. I have been playing games longer than I can remember, my first memory of gaming is trying to complete the entire 150cc section of Mario Kart while waiting for a delayed flight. I remember everyone around me being so miserable and unhappy, arguing with the poor lady at the desk while I was having the time of my life going through the levels (often getting stuck on the same map for multiple runs).

I love making games and being able to see your progress in real time makes game development so rewarding.

3D Graphics Programming

Programming using PS4 Dev kits in PSSL (Playstation Shader Language).

This project is covered by an NDA, this means that I cannot show anything to do with the programming.

This project imporved my ability to switch between and learn new languages and their conventions. I worked directly with shaders, handling input, generating normals, created different lighting types such as diffuse, ambient and specular. I created multiple graphics pipelines to mix materials and textures.

Raby Castle Consultancy Project

A simple drag and drop castle development game. The player can place the main hall of Raby Castle and then progress through time, unlocking more buildings at milestones which in turn allows the castle to grow and develop like it did through the past couple hundered years.

Gem Labyrinth Parkour

A short parkour level consisting of 5 distinct rooms the player must progress through: Starting Room, Forgery, Lava Lake, Success Room, and Super Success Room.

This level was developed in unreal to demonstrate my understanding of level design and showcase my ability to create a coherent environment.
