Finished Projects

Anything with Wheels 

I single-handedly developed a demo from a game idea, created as a group we decided on a game where players race unusual vehicles through common workplaces such as construction sites, offices, shops etc.

I created the game in unreal engine 5.6, I collected a handful of assets that matched the game idea and began constructing a level for the vehicles to race around. I worked in Blender to alter and rig the assets for better compatibility with the chaos vehicles add-on for unreal. I also utilised Photoshop to create some assets. 

https://zogsf.itch.io/anything-with-wheels 
 

3D Graphics Programming

Programming using PS4 Dev kits in PSSL (Playstation Shader Language).

This project is covered by an NDA, this means that I cannot show anything to do with the programming.

This project improved my ability to switch between and learn new languages and their conventions. I worked directly with shaders, handling input, generating normals. I created different lighting types such as diffuse, ambient and specular. I created multiple graphics pipelines to mix materials and textures.

Raby Castle Consultancy Project

A simple drag and drop castle development game. The player can place the main hall of Raby Castle and then progress through time, unlocking more buildings at milestones which in turn allows the castle to grow and develop like it did through the past couple hundered years.

 

Gem Labyrinth Parkour

A short parkour level consisting of 5 distinct rooms the player must progress through: Starting Room, Forgery, Lava Lake, Success Room, and Super Success Room.

This level was developed in unreal to demonstrate my understanding of level design and showcase my ability to create a coherent environment.
